Scylla

Scylla, Scourge of the Sea, began her career busking the Strait of Messina alongside her friend and longtime collaborator Charybdis. Between the two of them, they created a musical act so enchanting it was to die for. Despite making a successful living feasting on (the offerings of) sailors, she recently decided it was time for a change, gracing the stage of Oceanus with her angelic voice and two talented musicians at her back.

Jetty

Not much is known about Scylla's bandmates, as they closely guard any information on their private lives. There's no doubting their musical talent, however. The siren Jetty brings the harmony to the band - both through her backing vocals and her prowess on guitar and keys. Her stage presence is unmatched; she leaves no part of the crowd neglected, darting around the stage throughout the performance to make sure every audience member gets the same treatment from her guitar's punishing volume.

Roxy

Roxy is the siren that brings the band's rhythm. The power of her drumming provides the motor to the band's energetic rock anthems. On their acoustic tracks, she can be heard accompanying Scylla with her backing vocals and providing auxiliary percussion.

Special guest: Charybdis

Scylla's first collaborator returns for the band's biggest production yet - the Vow of Rivals series brings a new energy to the band's old favorites, and Charybdis can be heard providing her vocals to bring an extra punch for an unforgettable performance.
