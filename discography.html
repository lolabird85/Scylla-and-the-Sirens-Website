Hard Rock

  • Coral Crown

    • The band's rousing debut song; Scylla's vocals are so beautiful, they might just tempt you to jump over the side of your ship to get a better listen.

  • I Am Gonna Claw (Out Your Eyes Then Drown You to Death)

    • A catchy diss track directed at a certain vocal critic of the band's work. Never let them silence true art!

  • Rock and a Hard Place

    • A powerful anthem featuring special guest Charybdis providing some earth-splitting vocals!

  • Bewitching Eyes

    • A heartrending love song allegedly written by the band's guitarist, Jetty.



Acoustic Work

  • Song of the Sirens

    • A song depicting the tantalizing beauty of the ocean. A companion to Coral Crown.

  • Song of the Deep

    • A song advising the dangers of leaving familiar shores. A companion to I Am Gonna Claw (Out Your Eyes Then Drown You to Death).

  • Song of the Isles

    • A song about journeying to distant lands. A companion to Rock and a Hard Place.

  • Song of the Pearls

    • A song about seeking the ocean's bounties. A companion to Bewitching Eyes.
